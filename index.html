Welcome to PixelCity!
Why would I want to own a plot and a building in PixelCity?
For each plot/building you own, you get a share of royalties the city generates! The more residents move in, the higher land/building prices go and the more you earn.
How do I get started?
1. Purchase a plot of land and any building you like on PixelWhale. Click to Buy Plots/Buildings

2. Return to PixelCity and click your plot # (corresponds to the # you purchased on PixelWhale)

3. Use the Build function to put your building on the plot.

4. As a new owner, you’ll enjoy royalties as new residents move in and transfers are made. Occasionally check on your PixelCity plot(s) by clicking on them and then claim your rewards.
